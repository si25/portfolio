About

I'm a Front-end Developer with experience of building design-oriented responsive websites. I've mostly used CSS and JavaScript in the past but have also worked with Sass, Bootstrap, and React, as well as CMS's such as Squarespace, Shopify and WordPress.

I retrained as a Developer last year having previously worked in digital marketing and communications, and have since come to enjoy making websites that use clean UX/UI to clearly communicate ideas and information.

This site contains an assortment of personal projects and websites I've designed and built for others. I am currently available for freelance or contract work.
